Privacy Policy

It is diophantus Partners’ (“diophantus”) policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, diophantus collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. diophantus’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how diophantus’s visitors use its website. From time to time, diophantus may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

diophantus also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. diophantus does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to diophantus’s websites choose to interact with diophantus in ways that require diophantus to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that diophantus gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. diophantus does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Aggregated Statistics

diophantus may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. For instance, diophantus may monitor the most popular parts of the diophantuspartners.com site. diophantus may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, diophantus does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

diophantus discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on diophantus’s behalf, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Some of those employees, contractors and affiliated organizations may be located outside of your home country; by using diophantus’s websites, you consent to the transfer of such information to them. diophantus will not rent or sell potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information to anyone. Other than to its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations, as described above, diophantus discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only when required to do so by law, or when diophantus believes in good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the property or rights of diophantus, third parties or the public at large. If you are a registered user (such as a Limited Partner) of the diophantus website and have supplied your email address, diophantus may occasionally send you an email to tell you about new features, solicit your feedback, or just keep you up to date with diophantus. We primarily use our website to communicate this type of information, so we expect to keep this type of email to a minimum. If you send us a request (for example via a support email or via one of our feedback mechanisms), we reserve the right to publish it in order to help us clarify or respond to your request or to help us support other users. diophantus takes all measures reasonably necessary to protect against the unauthorized access, use, alteration or destruction of potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. diophantus uses cookies to help diophantus identify and track visitors, their usage of diophantus website, and their website access preferences. diophantus visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using diophantus’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of diophantus’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, diophantus may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and at diophantus’s sole discretion. diophantus encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
